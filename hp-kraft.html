About HP Kraft

HP Kraft (High Performance Kraft) is a specialized kraft paper known for its enhanced strength and durability. It is designed to offer superior performance in various demanding applications.

Key Features:

High Strength: Provides exceptional tensile strength and resistance to tearing, ideal for heavy-duty packaging.

Enhanced Durability: More resistant to moisture and abrasion, protecting contents even under tough conditions.

Superior Performance: Includes improved folding endurance and resistance to puncturing, enhancing overall performance.

Applications: Used in industrial and commercial packaging, suitable for heavy or sensitive items in sectors like shipping and manufacturing.

Material: Made from high-quality wood pulp using the kraft process, balancing strength and flexibility.
